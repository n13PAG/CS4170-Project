Switching Tires

The Interface

This is interface you will see during a race. It shows your track position, sector times and delta's between drivers

In the bottom right hand corner are two ways to communicate with your team and the respective buttons you need to press to open the communication menu

The Grid Tower

The Grid Tower shows the current lap, the deltas between drivers and the current tire type of the car.

The Team Radio Menu

This shows which buttons to press to access your team radio. If you have a mic you can access the voice commands. Alternatively you can open the communications menu

The Communication Menu

From this menu you can ask for information from your team.

You can choose which tires to switch to during the next pit stop

You can ask your to be ready for a pit stop this lap. You can do this by choosing the Box This Lap option
