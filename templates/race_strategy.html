Race Startegy

In F1 race strategy, teams aim to optimize factors like tire choices, pit stops, and fuel management. Prior to every you can choose the order and type of tires you will use as well as when you expect to switch. The strategy menu will show the expected track conditions and details about your strategy

Before a race your team will give an option between two strategies. The color in indicates the tire type and the line graph represents to expected tire wear over time.

Tire Wear

Teams switch tires due to tire wear. Tires will degrade over time due to friction from the track. More wear a tire has the less grip it has on the track making it harder to turn at speed and easier to lose control.

Uneven tire wear can make even easier to lose control of the car
