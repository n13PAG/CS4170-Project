Race Startegy

In F1, teams aim to optimize factors like tire choices, pit stops, and fuel management.

The strategy menu shows the expected track conditions and details about your strategy

Before a race your team will give you an option between two strategies. The color in indicates the tire type and the line graph represents to expected tire wear over time.

You can also edit a the strategy to your liking and choose which tires to use and when you plan to switch them in the race

Tire Wear

Teams switch tires due to tire wear. Tires will degrade over time due to friction from the track. The more wear a tire has the less grip it has on the track making it harder to turn at speed and easier to lose control.

Uneven tire wear can make even easier to lose control of the car
